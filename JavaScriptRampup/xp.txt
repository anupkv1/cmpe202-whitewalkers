XP Value : Courage In this week there was a big dilemma on what technology should we proceed with, Java or JavaScript, since some of my team mates want to follow a safer path for Java since they had good hands on, they were quite apprehensive in choosing JavaScript for this project. So i took the lead on sharing the tenets of JavaScript with my team mates and shared its vast scope in the field of development. Through all this motivation they got some courage.
